About Us

Ek Paryas Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to creating a just, inclusive, and empowered society. We work at the grassroots level to uplift underprivileged communities by addressing their basic needs and providing long-term support for sustainable development.

Our key focus areas include education for underprivileged children, women empowerment, support for poor families, environmental protection, and animal care. Through our initiatives, we aim to bring positive change and restore dignity to lives that need support the most.

We believe that small actions can create big impact. With the help of our volunteers, donors, and supporters, we continue to work towards building a compassionate society where everyone gets equal opportunities to grow and thrive.

Together, we can make a meaningful difference.

Our Key Programs

Education Support

Helping underprivileged children get access to quality education, books, and learning support.

Women Empowerment

Skill development, self-reliance training, and awareness programs to empower women socially and economically.

Environment Care

Tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and environmental awareness programs.

Helping Poor & Food Distribution

Providing food, ration kits, and basic necessities to poor and needy families.

Child Health & Nutrition

Supporting child health through nutrition, hygiene awareness, and basic healthcare support.

Animal Care & Dog Feeding

Feeding stray dogs, providing medical care, and spreading compassion towards animals.

Community Awareness Programs

Awareness campaigns on education, cleanliness, health, and social responsibility.
